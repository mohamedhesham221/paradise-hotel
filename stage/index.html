Our Hotel Is Nearby From The Most Important Tourist Areas

FLAME TOWERS ,BAKU

Flame Towers is a residential complex in Baku, Azerbaijan, with a construction cost of 350 million USD. It is one of the tallest buildings in Baku. Construction commenced in 2007 and was completed in 2012. The Flame Towers consist of 3 towers: the eastern tower, the west tower and the south tower. The buildings reach a height of 190 meters and the towers consist of apartments, administrative offices, a hotel and a shopping center.

OLD CITY ,BAKU

The ancient city or the inner city, which is an ancient historical area in the city of Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, which includes the Shirvanshahs' Palace and the Maiden's Tower, and has become part of the UNESCO World Heritage List and the construction of this city dates back to the seventh century AD.

HAYDER ALIJIF CENTER ,BAKU

Heydar Aliyev Center is one of the world-famous cultural centers, designed by Zaha Hadid. It is located in Baku, Azerbaijan. Zaha Hadid won the 2014 London Design Museum Award for this center.
